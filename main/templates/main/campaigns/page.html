Redistricting in Michigan

In 2021, a group of Michigan citizens will convene as part of the Independent Citizens Redistricting Commission to draw new state legislative and congressional districts. These district maps will inform how citizens vote, how much influence their votes have, and which politicians win elections to represent communities for the next 10 years!

It's important the these new district lines are fair and impartial. The best way to draw good district maps is to give the citizens on the Redistricting Commission a lot of public input from Michiganders like yourself!
