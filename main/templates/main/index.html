{% if LANGUAGE_CODE == 'en' %} What is Representable.org? {% elif LANGUAGE_CODE == 'es' %} ¿Qué es Representable.org? {% endif %}

We are a free, web-based platform designed to collect large-scale community data to support fair redistricting. Our tool offers actionable data & maps that show where Communities of Interest are located, and the economic and social interests that bind them together. This unprecedented digital data can support mapmakers, improve the public input process for marginalized communities, and support organizations with data to evaluate proposed maps. Join us to fight gerrymandering and mobilize all communities to get the representation they deserve. Learn more about gerrymandering and Representable.org in the Official Education Guide.
