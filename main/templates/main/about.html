Meet the team!

We’re a team of Princeton engineers who are excited to bring more technically advanced solutions to civic issues. Coming from a wide array of backgrounds, we put our technical and policy experience together to make a powerful crowdsourced tool which we hope can change the current polarized political landscape of how districts are made in the United States. We’re committed to giving a voice to those who have been marginalized or politically diluted by gerrymandering and look forward to turning Representable into a national tool for political equity.
